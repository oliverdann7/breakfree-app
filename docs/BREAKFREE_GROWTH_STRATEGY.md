## 1. Pre-Launch: Founding Users (Week 1–12 of Development)

### Goal

Build 500–1,000 founding users before public launch. These users will provide feedback, test features, and become community ambassadors.

### Tactics

#### 1.1 Influencer & Community Partnerships

**Timeline**: Week 1 of development

**Identify Micro-Influencers** (10K–100K followers)

- Fitness coaches
- Wellness advocates
- Health/lifestyle creators
- Meditation teachers
- Running clubs

**Approach**:

1. Send personalized email: "BreakFree is building X, would you be interested in early access?"
2. Offer: Free premium access for 1 year + feature naming rights
3. Ask for: Early beta testing + honest feedback
4. Expectation: Share journey with followers (1–2 posts/week during beta)

**Budget**: $5K–10K (1–2 influencers at ~$500–2K each)

**Expected Outcome**: 200–500 founding users

---

#### 1.2 Fitness & Wellness Community Outreach

**Timeline**: Week 4 of development

**Target Communities**:

- Turkish running clubs (Jogging Istanbul, Koşu Topluluğu)
- Yoga studios (Instagram following)
- Gym chains (link to members)
- Mental health forums (Reddit /r/turkey, Turkish blogs)
- Corporate wellness programs

**Approach**:

1. Direct outreach: "We're building a premium wellness app for Turkish users"
2. Offer: Free premium access for group members
3. Request: Share link with community
4. Provide: Referral tracking to measure impact

**Expected Outcome**: 300–500 founding users

## 4. Monetization: Premium Growth (Phase 2, Month 4+)

### Goal

10–20% of MAU converts to Premium; $10K+ MRR by Q1 2027

### Tactics

#### 4.1 Premium Positioning

**Tiers**:

1. **Free**: Browse talks, 1 challenge/month, limited mentor access
2. **Premium**: $29.99/month (~500 TRY)
   - Unlimited talks + recordings
   - 4 mentor sessions/month
   - Advanced health analytics
   - Challenge badges
   - Ad-free

**Paywall Placement**:

- 1st mentor booking
- After 10th talk
- On Day 7 (post-onboarding)
- In-app banner (non-intrusive)

**Trial Strategy**:

- 7-day free trial on sign-up
- No credit card required initially
- Reminder: "Your 3 days left" (on day 4)

---

#### 4.2 Pricing Strategy

**Research**:

- Compare: WHOOP ($39.99), Nike Training Club (free with premium), Insight Timer Premium ($14.99)
- Turkish market: Lower willingness to pay (~$10–20)
- Final price: $29.99/month or 99 TRY/month (~$3/month local pricing)

**Annual Discount**:

- Monthly: $29.99
- Annual: $249.99 (17% discount)
- Positioning: "Save $110 annually"

## 9. Crisis Management

### If Early Traction is Low (<500 users in Week 1)

1. **Diagnose**: Is it acquisition problem or product problem?
   - Check ASO (are we visible?)
   - Check retention (are users coming back?)
   - Check bugs (is app crashing?)

2. **Pivot quickly**:
   - Accelerate paid ads
   - Reach out to influencers personally
   - Deep dive into beta user feedback
   - Fix top 3 user-reported issues

3. **Alternative channels**:
   - Approach larger influencers (negotiate better deal)
   - Partner with gyms/yoga studios directly
   - Run corporate wellness pilots

---

### If Retention is Poor (<30% Day 7)

1. **Root cause analysis**:
   - Session recording + user testing (watch 5 users use app)
   - Check where users drop off
   - Analyze crash logs + error rates

2. **Immediate fixes** (could be):
   - Overwhelming UI → simplify onboarding
   - Bugs → fix crashes
   - Unclear value → improve talk discovery
   - No push notifications → turn on

3. **Communication**:
   - Email users who churned: "We'd love your feedback"
   - Offer $5 store credit for 1-min survey
   - Act on feedback quickly + tell users
