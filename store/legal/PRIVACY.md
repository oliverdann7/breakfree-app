# BreakFree — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-29
**Data controller:** BreakFree Türkiye (privacy@breakfree.tr)

This Privacy Policy describes how BreakFree ("we", "us", "the app") collects,
uses, stores, and shares personal data. We comply with the EU General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Turkish KVKK (Kişisel Verilerin Korunması
Kanunu, Law No. 6698).

## 1. Data we collect

| Category | Examples | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account | email, display name, avatar, language | you |
| Health metrics | sleep, steps, heart rate, hydration, mood | wearables (Apple Health, Google Fit, Garmin), manual entry |
| Community | posts, comments, likes, challenge participation | you |
| Mentor sessions | bookings, chat messages, notes, goals | you, mentors |
| Subscription | plan, renewal date, payment provider id | RevenueCat / Stripe / App Store / Play Store |
| Device | OS, app version, push token, IP | device |
| Analytics | screen views, feature usage | device |

We do not collect: payment card numbers (handled by Stripe/Apple/Google), exact location, contacts, microphone audio outside live talks.

## 2. How we use it

- Deliver the service (auth, sync, talks, mentorship, payments).
- Personalize recommendations (talks, mentors, challenges).
- Send notifications you've opted into.
- Compute wellness scores and badges.
- Diagnose crashes via Sentry (no PII in stack traces).

We do not sell your data. We do not use it to train third-party AI models.

## 3. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

- Contract: account, subscription, mentor bookings.
- Consent: health data, push notifications, analytics.
- Legitimate interest: security, fraud prevention, service improvement.

## 4. Sharing

| Recipient | Purpose | Region |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Firebase / Google Cloud | hosting, auth, storage | EU + USA |
| RevenueCat / Stripe / Apple / Google | payments | EU + USA |
| Agora.io | live audio for talks | global |
| Sentry | crash diagnostics | EU |
| Expo | push delivery | USA |

International transfers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where applicable.

## 5. Retention

- Account + profile: until deletion request + 30 days cool-off.
- Health metrics: 24 months rolling.
- Chat messages: until deletion or 5 years.
- Audit logs: 12 months.

## 6. Your rights (KVKK Art. 11 / GDPR Art. 15–22)

- Access: in-app `Profile → Privacy → Data export` (JSON within 24h).
- Rectification: edit profile.
- Deletion: in-app `Profile → Privacy → Delete account`. 30-day cool-off.
- Restriction / objection: contact privacy@breakfree.tr.
- Portability: data export above.
- Withdraw consent: in app settings.

You may file a complaint with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Authority
(KVK Kurumu) or your local EU supervisory authority.

## 7. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (Firebase default
encryption). Push tokens, OAuth refresh tokens, and Stripe customer ids are
stored only server-side. We follow the OWASP MASVS for mobile.

## 8. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
data from children. Contact us if you believe we have.

## 9. Changes

We will notify you in-app of material changes 14 days before they take effect.
